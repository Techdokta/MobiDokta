How It Works for Students

Repair without leaving campus.

Use your network. Use your transport. Let us handle the rest.

1

WhatsApp Us

Send your student number, campus, device type, and what's wrong. We'll confirm same-day availability and send your quote immediately.

2

Send Via a Friend

Ask a flatmate, classmate, or anyone heading to Pretoria West / Danville to drop it at Shop 6, Madeira Isles Shopping Complex. Takes 2 minutes to hand in.

3

Or Use Your Campus Transport

Many campus shuttles and Gauteng transit routes pass through Pretoria West. Your shuttle driver or fellow passengers can drop the device on your behalf — we just need your name and WhatsApp number on a note attached.

4

We Fix While You Study

Your device goes straight to the bench. We diagnose, quote, repair, and test — all while you're in lectures. You get WhatsApp updates at every stage.

5

Your Friend Collects

Same person who dropped it off collects it on their way back. Or we courier it back to your campus address. Your device is fully fixed, tested, and under warranty.

Getting Here

Routes from your campus to Madeira Isles.

Shop 6, Madeira Isles Shopping Complex, 262 Klitsgras St, Danville, Pretoria — right off the main Pretoria West corridors.

TUT Pretoria Campus

~8 km · ~18 min drive

The Staatsartillerie Road / Thabo Sehume St route connects TUT Pretoria directly to Danville. Many TUT students travel through Pretoria West daily. Ask in your res WhatsApp group — someone's heading this way.

TUT Soshanguve

~28 km · ~35 min drive

The R80 / Mabopane Highway runs through Pretoria West. Students traveling from Soshanguve to Pretoria CBD pass near Danville. Intercampus shuttles frequently use this route.

UP Hatfield

~12 km · ~22 min drive

Flat route via Church Street / West Avenue through Pretoria CBD to Danville. UP student taxis often travel westward. Check UP Res WhatsApp groups for someone heading to Pretoria West or Danville.

UNISA (Muckleneuk)

~7 km · ~15 min drive

UNISA is one of the closest campuses to Danville. Muckleneuk sits just east of Pretoria West — a direct 15-minute route via Potgieter St or the N1. Ideal for couriers too.

Sefako Makgatho (SMU)

~30 km · ~40 min drive

Students from Ga-Rankuwa / Medunsa travel the R80 or N4 into Pretoria. Danville is accessible before hitting the CBD — so your friend can drop it on the way in without adding time to their commute.

Rosebank College · Boston · Varsity College · Damelin

Pretoria CBD campuses · ~8–12 km

Most Pretoria CBD private colleges are 10–15 minutes from Danville. A quick Bolt or shared taxi gets you here and back in 45 minutes. Or drop off on your commute to campus in the morning.

TUT Ga-Rankuwa

~32 km · ~40 min

R80 corridor through Pretoria West. Students commuting into Pretoria regularly pass Danville. Check campus ride-sharing groups — someone drops off in the morning, collects in the afternoon.

Rosebank International · Stadio · CTI · Richfield

Various Pretoria locations · 10–20 min

If your campus is in Hatfield, Arcadia, Sunnyside, or the CBD — Danville is a straight westward drive. Day students with cars can drop and collect the same day with no disruption to their schedule.

The 60-Minute Recovery Protocol

Studies show recovery rates drop significantly after one hour. Do these four steps in order — right now, before anything else.

The first 60 minutes are critical. Device thieves either sell the phone quickly to a fence, or turn it off to kill tracking. Speed is your advantage.
1

Lock it remotely — mark as Lost

Open Find My (Apple) or Find My Device (Google / Samsung SmartThings) on any device or browser. Mark the phone as Lost. This locks the screen to a custom message + contact number, disables Apple Pay / Google Pay, and continues tracking even if the SIM is swapped (iPhone with Wi-Fi nearby).

iCloud Find My → Google Find My →
⚠️ Do NOT erase yet — that kills tracking and your evidence
2

Block the SIM — call your carrier NOW

Contact your mobile network and request an immediate SIM block. Give them your phone number and SA ID. This prevents the thief using your account, running up data costs, or intercepting your OTP banking codes.

Vodacom: 082 111  ·  MTN: 083 173 0000  ·  Telkom: 081 180  ·  Cell C: 084 135

⚠️ Also call your bank's fraud line if you use mobile banking — SIM theft often targets banking OTPs
3

Get a SAPS case number

Report the theft to SAPS — you need a case number for your insurance claim, IMEI blacklisting, and for our network to act. You can start a report online at MySAPS for theft, or walk into any SAPS station.

MySAPS online report →
Keep the case number — you'll need it for steps 4 and for your insurer
4

WhatsApp MobiDokta your IMEI number (found on your box, receipt, or in Settings before theft) + your SAPS case number. We immediately circulate the IMEI and device description to our partner network — second-hand dealers, pawn shops, and PSIRA-registered security firms in Pretoria and Tshwane.

SIM Swap Assistance

Lost your SIM? Back online fast.

A SIM swap (SIM replacement) gets you a new SIM card with your existing number. We guide you through the quickest path with every SA carrier — or help you activate an eSIM instead.

Vodacom

SIM Replacement

Bring your SA ID + account number to any Vodacom store or MyMTN app. New SIM issued same day. SIM swap can also be done via the Vodacom app with selfie verification if your account is in good standing.

MTN

SIM Replacement

MTN requires a RICA re-registration at their store with SA ID. If your SIM was stolen, bring your SAPS case number — speeds up the process. MyMTN app also supports SIM swap with facial verification.

Telkom Mobile

SIM Replacement

Visit a Telkom store with your SA ID and account number. If you don't know your account PIN, your ID is sufficient. New SIM is usually activated within 2 hours.

Cell C

SIM Replacement

Cell C SIM swap can be done at retail stores or via the Cell C app with SA ID. Bring a SAPS report if the SIM was stolen — this waives some verification requirements.

eSIM — iPhone 12+

Go SIM-Free

iPhone 12 and newer supports eSIM. Instead of a physical SIM, your carrier sends a QR code you scan directly on the phone. MobiDokta can assist with eSIM setup on any supported iPhone — no carrier store visit needed in most cases.

⚠️ Was your SIM swapped WITHOUT your consent?

SIM swap fraud is common in South Africa — criminals impersonate you at a carrier store or exploit app verification gaps to take over your phone number. They then receive your banking OTPs and drain accounts. If you suddenly have no signal and can't explain why:

  • Call your bank immediately — freeze all transactions. Do NOT wait.
  • Call your carrier — report the fraudulent swap and get your number back.
  • File a SAPS report — criminal case under the Cybercrimes Act.
  • Report to the NCA (National Credit Act) and SAFPS — helps block further credit fraud.
  • WhatsApp MobiDokta — we help with IMEI blacklisting and documentation.

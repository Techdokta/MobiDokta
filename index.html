Past Work & Client Love

Cured & Discharged Cases.

A glimpse into the surgical precision of our TechDoktas and what our clients have to say.

Before

iPhone 11 — Shattered Display · Danville, Pretoria

Cracked glass doesn't always mean a dead LCD. We test touch and display function first — if touch still responds, an OEM-grade replacement can save you hundreds over a full assembly. Tip: Stop pressing on a cracked screen — pressure spreads the fracture into the LCD layers underneath, turning a cheaper fix into a far more expensive one.

After

iPhone 11 — Screen Replaced · MobiDokta, Pretoria West

Fitted with an OEM-grade panel — factory colour accuracy, no brightness mismatch, no aftermarket green tint. iPhone screen repairs in Pretoria West from R599 (July Sale). Tip: Always ask whether you're getting Incell LCD, Hard OLED, or Soft OLED — they are not the same quality and the price gap reflects it.

Fixed

iPhone 13 — Battery Replacement · Pretoria

Battery health had dropped to 67% — charging to 100% took over 3 hours and the phone shut down unexpectedly under load. Replaced with an OEM-grade cell in 45 minutes. Tip: Replace your iPhone battery when health falls below 80%. Below that threshold, iOS actively throttles CPU speed to prevent unexpected shutdowns — your phone feels slow because it is being slowed down intentionally.

Saved

iPhone — Water Damage Board Recovery · Pretoria West

Dropped in a pool. Client arrived 48 hours later — phone still in a bag of rice. We performed ultrasonic logic board cleaning and component-level corrosion removal and fully revived it. Tip: Rice absorbs zero moisture from inside a phone. The moment your phone gets wet, power it off, don't charge it, and bring it in within 24 hours. Every hour of delay increases corrosion — and your data loss risk.

Restored

iPhone 14 Pro Max — Back Glass · Danville Workshop

The titanium frame survived the drop perfectly but the ceramic back glass shattered. We separated the panel using a precision laser heating system and fitted a factory-spec replacement. Tip: MagSafe charging and wireless accessories continue working perfectly after a quality back glass replacement — don't let any technician tell you otherwise. Cheap glue-only jobs are the ones that fail.

Fixed

iPhone — Camera Repair · MobiDokta, Danville Pretoria

A corner drop cracked the sapphire lens protector, causing blurry photos, autofocus hunting, and a noticeable purple haze in direct light. We replaced the rear camera assembly in under an hour. Tip: A R89 camera lens protector prevents a R649+ camera module replacement. The lens on your iPhone is precision ground sapphire glass — one uncovered drop and it scratches permanently. Add one before you leave the shop.

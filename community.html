Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to your most pressing repair questions.

OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) Grade parts are identical in specification and performance to the parts originally installed in your device. They offer the highest reliability, exact color accuracy (for screens), and maximum longevity. Eco-grade parts are high-quality aftermarket alternatives that provide excellent functionality at a lower price point, though they may have slight variations in maximum brightness or color temperature.

Absolutely. At MobiDokta, we operate under strict POPIA compliance. Our engineers do not access your personal files. For hardware repairs (like screen or battery replacements), your data remains untouched. However, we always strongly recommend performing a full backup of your device before handing it over, just as a standard precaution.

Most standard procedures, such as battery or screen replacements for iPhones and Samsung devices, are completed within 1 to 2 hours of drop-off. Complex micro-soldering, logic board repairs, or water damage recovery can take between 24 to 72 hours depending on the severity of the damage.

Yes. All OEM grade part replacements come with a comprehensive 6-month warranty covering manufacturing defects. Eco-grade parts carry a 3-month warranty. Please note that warranties are voided if the device suffers subsequent physical damage (e.g., dropping it again) or liquid damage.

POPIA Privacy Policy

In compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa, MobiDokta is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is collected and used properly, lawfully, and transparently.

Data Collection & Usage

  • We collect only necessary information (Name, Contact Number, Email, Device IMEI/Serial) required to fulfill our repair contract with you.
  • Your data is never sold to third parties.
  • Physical devices are kept securely in our 24/7 monitored facility and data on the device is strictly confidential.

Terms & Conditions of Service

By booking a repair or utilizing MobiDokta's services, you agree to our comprehensive Terms and Conditions. These rules are designed to protect both the client and the company.

Key Provisions

  • Liability: While we treat every device with engineering-grade care, MobiDokta is not liable for data loss. Clients must back up devices prior to service.

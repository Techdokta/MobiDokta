Cookie Policy

How MobiDokta uses cookies and your rights under POPIA.

Last updated: June 2026

1. Who we are

MobiDokta is a device repair service operated by HyperMartX Tech (Pty) Ltd (Reg. K2025/218955/07), registered on the Central Supplier Database (MAAA1688961). Our website is www.mobidokta.co.za.

We are a responsible party under the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 (POPIA). This policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies on our website.

2. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to remember information about your visit — such as your language preference or whether you have consented to analytics tracking.

3. What cookies we use

Category Cookie / Technology Purpose Can you opt out? Duration
Essential md_consent_v1 (localStorage) Stores your cookie consent decision so the banner does not reappear on every page visit. No — required for the consent system to function Until you clear browser data
Analytics Google Analytics 4 (_ga, _ga_*) Tracks page views, session duration, and button clicks (including WhatsApp booking clicks) to help us understand how visitors use the site. No personal data is sold or shared with third parties for marketing. Yes — via the consent banner Up to 2 years

We do not use advertising cookies, social media tracking pixels, or any other third-party tracking beyond Google Analytics.

4. Legal basis under POPIA

Under POPIA's Processing Limitation condition, we may only process personal information with your consent or for a legitimate purpose that does not require consent.

  • Essential cookies — processed under legitimate interest (the site cannot function without them). No consent required.
  • Analytics cookies — processed only with your explicit, affirmative consent. Google Analytics does not load unless you click "Yes, all good" or enable the analytics toggle in the consent panel.

5. Your rights

Under POPIA, you have the right to:

  • Be informed — this policy fulfils that obligation.
  • Object to processing — click "Essentials only" on the consent banner, or clear your browser's site data to remove your stored consent and be shown the banner again.
  • Withdraw consent — clear your browser's localStorage/cookies for mobidokta.co.za at any time. Analytics tracking will stop immediately on your next visit.
  • Access your data — contact us at techrepair@mobidokta.co.za. We hold only a consent timestamp (no name, no email) in our server logs.

6. Cross-border data transfers

Google Analytics stores data on Google's servers, which are located outside of South Africa (primarily the United States). This cross-border transfer is permitted under POPIA where the recipient country or organisation offers adequate protection. Google's Data Processing Terms include standard contractual clauses that satisfy this requirement.

7. Data retention

Google Analytics data is retained for 26 months by default in our GA4 property. Our consent audit log is retained for 5 years in accordance with POPIA best practices (duration of processing plus 3 years).

8. Third parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party for marketing purposes. The only third party receiving data from your visit is Google (via Analytics), and only if you have consented.

DJI repair services we cover

Gimbal Repair & Calibration

Shaky footage, gimbal overload errors, broken gimbal arm, or camera tilting on Mavic, Mini, Air, and Phantom series. Motor replacement and IMU calibration.

Propeller Motor Replacement

Bent, burnt, or seized motors after a crash. Full motor swap — all axes. We test thrust balance after replacement to ensure stable flight.

Crash Damage Assessment

Post-crash inspection: structural frame damage, sensor calibration, ESC faults, motor balance, and flight controller diagnostics.

Signal & Controller Issues

Disconnected controller, weak signal range, or OcuSync/RC-N1 pairing failures. Antenna checks, firmware reflash, and controller board diagnosis.

Battery & Charging Issues

DJI Intelligent Flight Battery not charging, swollen, or showing critical error codes. Battery cell diagnosis and charging hub port repair.

Water & Dust Damage

Flew through rain or dropped in water? Board-level cleaning and corrosion removal before the damage becomes permanent.

Osmo Action & Pocket Repair

DJI Osmo Action cracked lens, broken screen, or gimbal failure. DJI Osmo Pocket stabilisation faults and connectivity issues.

Flight Controller & ESC Repair

Compass errors, IMU calibration failures, ESC beeping faults. Board-level repair for total loss of flight stability or persistent error codes.

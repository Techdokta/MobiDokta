iMac services we cover

Screen Replacement

Cracked Retina display, dead pixels, yellow tint, or backlight failure. Panel replacement for iMac 24" M-series and all 21.5" / 27" Intel iMac models.

SSD Upgrade & Replacement

Slow iMac or failing storage? SSD upgrade for Intel iMac 2019–2020. Data migration included. Bring your iMac in — we carry it out the same day where possible.

RAM Upgrade (Intel iMac)

Intel iMac 27" supports user-accessible RAM upgrades up to 128GB. We source, install, and test your new RAM. Improve performance without replacing the machine.

Overheating & Thermal Clean

iMac fans running at full speed, system sluggish under load, or randomly shutting down. Full thermal disassembly, dust extraction, and thermal compound refresh.

Liquid Damage Recovery

Liquid spilled into or around your iMac. Ultrasonic board cleaning, corrosion treatment, and component-level diagnosis — before permanent damage sets in.

No Power / Won't Boot

iMac completely dead or stuck on the Apple logo. PSU diagnosis, board-level fault isolation, and T2 / M-chip recovery diagnostics.

Logic Board Repair

Kernel panics, GPU artefacts, random shutdowns, or total board failure. Micro-soldering and component-level repair for Intel and M-series iMac logic boards.

Port & Connectivity Repair

Thunderbolt 4, USB-A, USB-C, or Ethernet port not working. Board-level port repair or hub replacement. iMac won't recognise external drives or monitors.
